EmpowHer Short Essay Submission

Write your answers to for following questions in the corresponding textboxes. Each question has a word limit of 500 words. For more information, see the EmpowHer Submission Guidelines.

Each team should only submit ONE copy of your responses. If another member of your team has already submitted this form, please do not submit it again.

Please answer the follwing questions and upload a PDF document of your responses. Please limit your responses to 5MB.

A) Explain the problem you are aiming to solve and discuss the deficits of existing solutions on the market.

B) Discuss your final prototype and how its feature set separates it from other solutions on the market. Include a description of the technologies you utilized to create each part of the prototype.

C) What are the key skillsets you learned during the design process? How did you collaborate with your mentor and team to strengthen the project over the past months?

D) Include any other supplemental information that will help the judges understand your project.
